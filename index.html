Priority Actions

1. Cut Winter Labor Costs

November & December labor is 54-72% of revenue vs 25-35% industry benchmark. Potential savings: €2,500-3,500/month.

2. Remove 13 Dog Products

Low popularity + low margin products. Only €442 revenue at risk (1.8% of total). Simplifies menu & reduces waste.

3. Promote Coffee Products

Coffee has 75% margin vs 50% on meals. Push espresso (€945), Meia Leite (€1,025), Cappuccino (€425) for better profitability.
